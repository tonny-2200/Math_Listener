How to Use Math Listener
  • Square Brackets []: Say "open squared brackets" to open and "closed squared brackets" to close.
  • Parenthesis (): Say "open parenthesis" to open and "closed parenthesis" to close.
  • Permutation ⁿPₖ: Say "n permutation k" to express.
  • Combination ⁿCₖ: Say "n combination k" to express.
  • Exponents:
    • Use "raised to" or "to the power" for exponents (e.g., "2 raised to 3").
    • Say "number square" or "number cube" for square (²) or cube (³).
  • Integration: Say "lower limit integral upper limit" to print limits like ₄∫⁵.
  • Modulo: Say "modulo your expression modulo" to open and close the modulo expression.
